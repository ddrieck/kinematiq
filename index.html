OUR STORY

A team of innovators, creators and extreme sports athletes invent and implement cutting edge technology that challenges the unknown in action sports. Action defines us, we are powered by passion, we are powered by personal progression.

Kinematiq was founded by three dynamic, passionate and committed extreme athletes in the quest to push the boundaries of their sport and create realistic competition amongst themselves. The founders Leo (Germany), Johnny (France) and Yzten (Netherlands) met at a local kiteboarding spot, Pleasure Bay in Boston, many years ago. Their competitive nature compelled them to question how one could quantify how high one jumped. After 2 months searching for a functional and reliable motion-sensing device, they decided to combine their skills, quit their current jobs and create a motion sensing technology that will enable them to redefine action sports as they knew it.

With an attachable, intuitively designed technology you can turn your board into more than just sports equipment…
